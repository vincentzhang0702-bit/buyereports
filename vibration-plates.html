DECISION GUIDE

How to choose a vibration plate

Focus on the features that match your goals — platform size, speed range, and build quality make the biggest difference in daily use.

01

Platform Size

Larger platforms provide more stability and room to move during workouts.

02

Speed Range

Wider speed ranges (1–120 levels) give you more control over intensity.

03

Weight Capacity

Look for stated capacities of 300+ lbs for durability and broader usability.

04

Features & Controls

Remote controls, Bluetooth, and preset programs add convenience to routines.
