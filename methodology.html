OUR METHODOLOGY

A ranking should explain itself.

Every BuyeReports category uses a defined decision framework designed around how U.S. shoppers compare that product.

Vibration plate scoring framework

30%Platform, stability and stated capacity
25%Controls, useful modes and usability
20%Home fit, noise, storage and portability
15%Warranty, support and ownership experience
10%Current U.S. value and availability

Sources we may use

Manufacturer specifications and manuals, retailer listings, verified customer feedback, warranty and support information, pricing and availability, product history and relevant market comparisons.

Commercial relationships

A product may be sold by a commercial partner. Partner status is labeled separately from editorial scoring. Commercial terms may determine whether a purchase link is available, but they do not create factual product claims.

Updates and corrections

Rankings are reviewed when products, prices, availability or source information change. Readers and brands may submit corrections through our contact page; material corrections are reviewed before publication.
